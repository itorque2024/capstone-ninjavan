Topic: Failed Delivery

If your delivery attempt failed, NinjaVan will automatically re-attempt delivery on the next business day. You will receive an SMS and email notification. NinjaVan makes up to 3 delivery attempts total. After 3 failed attempts, the parcel is returned to the sender. To reschedule before the next attempt, visit ninjavan.co/en-sg/tracking and select Reschedule, or contact NinjaVan via NinjaChat (Telegram: https://t.me/NinjaVanBot). You may also redirect your parcel to a Ninja Point for self-collection — over 800 collection points island-wide.
