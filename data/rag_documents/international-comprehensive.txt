CUSTOMS AND DUTIES:
- All international shipments subject to customs inspection at the destination.
- Import duties and taxes are the recipient's responsibility (default — DDU, Delivered Duty Unpaid).
- DDP (Delivered Duty Paid) available for Malaysia, Indonesia, Philippines, Thailand, Vietnam — duties paid upfront by sender for a smoother recipient experience.
- Accurate customs declaration is mandatory. Undervaluing or misdeclaring is illegal and voids insurance.

CUSTOMS DOCUMENT REQUIREMENTS:
- Commercial invoice (value, description, quantity)
- Packing list
- Certificate of Origin (for selected product categories)
- Permits if required (food, health products, electronics)

INTERNATIONAL SHIPPING RATES:
- Volumetric weight applies: (L × W × H in cm) ÷ 5000 = volumetric weight in kg
- Billing uses whichever is higher: actual weight or volumetric weight
- Indicative starting rates from Singapore: Malaysia from S$5, Indonesia from S$8, Hong Kong from S$10, Philippines from S$8, Thailand/Vietnam from S$8, Australia/UK/USA from S$20
- Rates depend on parcel weight, dimensions, and service level

TRACKING INTERNATIONAL PARCELS:
- Track at ninjavan.co/en-sg/tracking using your NV tracking number.
- Once handed to the local delivery partner in the destination country, tracking continues on the partner network.
- Tracking may slow during customs clearance (normal: 1–5 business days).

CUSTOMS CLEARANCE DELAYS:
- If held by customs, NinjaVan will notify you via email.
- You may need to provide additional documents or pay import duties.
- Customs delays are outside NinjaVan's control and do not restart the SLA clock.

INTERNATIONAL CLAIMS:
- Lost international parcels: contact shippercare_sg@ninjavan.co promptly.
- Damaged international parcels: contact within 7 days of actual delivery.
- International claims subject to destination country liability limits.
- NinjaVan Shield insurance strongly recommended for high-value international shipments.
