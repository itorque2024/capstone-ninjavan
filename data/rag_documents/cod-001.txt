Topic: Cash on Delivery (COD)

NinjaVan supports Cash on Delivery (COD) for eligible orders. The rider collects payment on behalf of the merchant when the parcel is delivered. Accepted payment method: cash (SGD). PayNow QR is also supported by participating merchants. Please prepare the exact amount — riders carry limited change. If you cannot pay the COD amount at delivery, the parcel will be returned to the sender. Contact the seller to arrange payment before the next delivery attempt. COD collected by NinjaVan is consolidated and remitted to merchants at end of the month after calculations and verification. A handling fee of S$1 or 3% of the invoice value (whichever is higher) is deducted before remittance.
