Topic: Prohibited Items

NinjaVan does not ship: weapons and ammunition, live animals, illegal drugs and narcotics, explosives and fireworks, radioactive materials, toxic chemicals, counterfeit goods, human remains or biological samples, and items prohibited by the destination country. Perishable fresh food is only permitted on same-day or next-day services with proper packaging and dry ice declared. Lithium batteries are allowed inside devices; standalone batteries must be individually wrapped, declared, and are limited to 2 per shipment. Attempting to ship prohibited items may result in parcel seizure, account termination, or legal action under Singapore customs and transport law. When in doubt, email shippercare_sg@ninjavan.co before shipping.
