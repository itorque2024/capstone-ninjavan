Topic: Merchant and Seller Services

NINJAVAN MERCHANT DASHBOARD:
URL: dashboard.ninjavan.co
The merchant dashboard is the all-in-one platform for managing shipments, tracking, and analytics.
Key features:
- Order creation (manual or bulk CSV upload)
- API integration for automated order creation
- Real-time tracking for all shipments
- COD remittance reports and reconciliation
- Return and RMA management
- Proof of delivery (POD) bulk download
- Failed delivery management and re-attempt scheduling

SERVICE TIERS:
- Ninja Flexi: Pay-as-you-go, no account required, starts from S$3.49/parcel
- Shopify self-sign-up: starts from S$3.90/parcel
- Ninja Packs: Prepaid poly mailers from S$3.40/piece (packaging + delivery included)
- Eco Ninja Packs: 100% biodegradable mailers, incineration-safe
- Ninja Dash: For merchants >150 parcels/month — next-day delivery, custom pricing
- Enterprise: Custom SLA and pricing for high-volume shippers; dedicated account manager

GETTING STARTED:
1. Register at dashboard.ninjavan.co (free account)
2. Verify business documents (ACRA registration for Singapore businesses)
3. Choose a service tier
4. Integrate via API or use the dashboard for manual bookings
5. Set up monthly invoicing or Ninja Flexi pay-per-shipment billing

API INTEGRATION:
- RESTful API for automated shipment creation, tracking, and webhook events
- Supported platforms: Shopify, WooCommerce, Lazada, Shopee, TikTok Shop, eBay, Zalora
- API documentation available via dashboard

PICKUP SERVICE (MERCHANT):
- Schedule pickup date when creating order on dashboard
- Pickups occur between 9 AM and 10 PM on the selected date
- Cannot schedule same-day pickup at time of order creation
- Surcharge: S$5 for pickups with fewer than 5 parcels per trip
- Ninja Packs shipments are NOT eligible for pickup — must drop off at a Ninja Point

DROP-OFF:
- 800+ Ninja Points island-wide (see ninjavan.co/en-sg/support/find-ninja-point)
- Standard size limit: under 5 kg and 80 cm combined (L+W+H)
- Selected locations accept larger parcels — use the map filter to find them

BILLING:
- Invoice sent monthly via email for the previous month's deliveries
- Payment via bank/wire transfer or PayNow
- Include invoice number in payment reference
- Late payment: dashboard access suspended; restored within 1 working day of payment
- Disputes: email shippercare_sg@ninjavan.co; successful disputes result in a credit note

COD REMITTANCE FOR MERCHANTS:
- COD collected consolidated at end of the month and remitted after calculations and verification
- Handling fee: S$1 OR 3% of invoice value, whichever is higher — deducted before remittance
- Remittance via bank transfer or PayNow to registered business account
- Failed COD collections flagged in shipment reports on the dashboard

NINJAVAN SHIELD (INSURANCE):
- Optional per-parcel add-on at time of booking
- Standard Shield: up to S$2,000 or declared value, whichever is lower
- Premium Shield: up to S$10,000 — contact your account manager
- Claims: email shippercare_sg@ninjavan.co

RETURN TO SENDER (RTS):
- After 3 failed delivery attempts, parcel returned to sender
- Dashboard shows RTS status with reason code
- RTS parcels held at depot — contact support to arrange redelivery or disposal

FULFILLMENT AND WAREHOUSING:
- Warehouses in SG, MY, ID, VN, PH
- Services: storage, pick and pack, sorting, kitting
- Pay-per-use model
- Integrates with Shopee, Lazada, Shopify, TikTok Shop, eBay, Zalora
