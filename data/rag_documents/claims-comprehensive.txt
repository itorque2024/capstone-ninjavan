Topic: Claims — Damage, Loss, and Compensation

HOW TO FILE A CLAIM:
Email shippercare_sg@ninjavan.co with:
- Your tracking number
- Description of the claim type: Damaged Item, Lost Parcel, or Wrong Item Delivered
- Supporting documents (see below)

HOW TO CONTACT: Email shippercare_sg@ninjavan.co or via NinjaChat (Telegram: https://t.me/NinjaVanBot, Mon–Sat 9 AM–8 PM SGT).

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS BY CLAIM TYPE:

Damaged Item:
- Tracking number
- Clear photos of the damaged item (all sides)
- Clear photos of the damaged outer packaging and inner packaging
- Purchase receipt or invoice showing item value
- For electronics: photo of serial number

Lost Parcel:
- Tracking number
- Proof of purchase (receipt or invoice)
- Description of contents and declared value

Wrong Item Delivered:
- Tracking number
- Photo of item received
- Photo of the packaging label
- Original order confirmation showing what was ordered

COMPENSATION POLICY:

Standard Coverage (included in all shipments):
- Covers lost or damaged items up to the declared value

NinjaVan Shield (optional add-on, purchased at time of booking):
- Standard Shield: up to S$2,000 or declared value, whichever is lower
- Premium Shield: up to S$10,000 — contact your account manager for enterprise accounts

NOT COVERED:
- Damage due to improper or insufficient packaging by the sender
- Fragile items not declared or insufficiently packaged (e.g., glass, ceramics without proper packing)
- Perishable goods spoiled during transit
- Prohibited items
- Consequential losses (loss of business, lost profits)
- Items with no proof of value

PACKAGING NOTE: Poly mailers provide little to no protection. Fragile items must be individually wrapped in at least 8 cm of bubble wrap inside a double-walled corrugated box. Items not meeting packaging standards are not covered by NinjaVan's damage policy.

TIPS FOR A SUCCESSFUL CLAIM:
- File promptly after discovering the issue.
- Photos must clearly show the damage — blurry or partial images may delay the review.
- Keep all original packaging until the claim is fully resolved.
- The declared value at time of booking sets the maximum compensation limit.
