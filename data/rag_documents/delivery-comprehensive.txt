Topic: Delivery — Complete Policy and Process

DELIVERY ATTEMPTS:
NinjaVan makes up to 3 delivery attempts for every parcel.
- Attempt 1: On the scheduled delivery date during the preferred window.
- Attempt 2: Next business day if the first attempt fails.
- Attempt 3: Following business day if the second attempt fails.
After 3 failed attempts, the parcel is returned to sender (Return to Sender / RTS).
Weekends and public holidays are not counted as business days for re-attempt purposes.

FAILED DELIVERY — COMMON REASONS:
- No one present at delivery address during the delivery window.
- Address incomplete, incorrect, or inaccessible.
- Recipient refused delivery.
- Parcel too large to leave safely without recipient present.
- Rider unable to reach recipient via SMS or call.

WHAT TO DO AFTER A FAILED DELIVERY:
1. Check your SMS/email for the failed delivery notification.
2. Visit ninjavan.co/en-sg/tracking → Reschedule Delivery (must be done at least 2 hours before the next attempt window).
3. Alternatively, redirect to a Ninja Point for self-collection (800+ locations island-wide).
4. To change delivery address, contact NinjaVan support — a fee may apply.

SAFE DROP / LEAVE AT DOOR:
- Authorise NinjaVan to leave the parcel at your door when you are unavailable.
- Rider captures a photo as proof of delivery.
- Once left, liability passes to the recipient.
- Not available for COD, fragile, or high-value parcels.

DELIVERY ADDRESS CHANGE:
- Must be requested before the parcel reaches Out for Delivery status.
- Address must be within the same delivery zone.
- Contact NinjaVan support via NinjaChat (Telegram: https://t.me/NinjaVanBot) or email shippercare_sg@ninjavan.co.

NINJA POINTS / PARCEL COLLECTION:
- 800+ Ninja Points (PUDO partners) across Singapore.
- Find locations at ninjavan.co/en-sg/support/find-ninja-point.
- Bring tracking number and government-issued ID.
- Present OTP + QR code from your collection notification SMS.
- Standard parcels held for a limited number of days before return to sender.

SLA — DELIVERY TIMEFRAMES (Singapore):
- Standard: 1–3 working days from pickup
- Next-day: Available only for Ninja Dash shippers (>150 parcels/month)
- International: Varies by country — see international shipping guide

PEAK PERIOD DELAYS:
During 11.11 Sale, 12.12 Sale, Eid al-Fitr, Chinese New Year, and Christmas, SLAs may extend by 1–3 business days. NinjaVan proactively notifies affected customers via SMS.

CONTACTLESS DELIVERY:
All NinjaVan deliveries support contactless handover. No signature required unless parcel is high-value or COD. A photo is taken as proof at every delivery.
