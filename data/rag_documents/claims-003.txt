Topic: Claims Timeline and Compensation

To file a claim, email shippercare_sg@ninjavan.co with your tracking number and supporting evidence (photos, receipt, invoice). File as soon as possible after the issue is discovered. Standard coverage is included in all NinjaVan shipments and covers lost or damaged parcels up to the declared value. For enhanced protection, NinjaVan Shield is an optional add-on purchased at the time of booking — it covers up to S$2,000 (standard) or up to S$10,000 (Premium Shield, available for enterprise merchants). Approved claims are paid via bank transfer. Items damaged due to improper packaging by the sender are not eligible for compensation.
