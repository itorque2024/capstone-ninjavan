Topic: Parcel Tracking — Complete Guide

NinjaVan Tracking Portal: ninjavan.co/en-sg/tracking
Tracking number format: NV-XXXXXX (printed on airway bill and in shipping confirmation email/SMS)
Tracking updates refresh approximately every 30 minutes.

TRACKING STATUSES EXPLAINED:
- Pending Pickup: Parcel not yet collected from sender.
- Picked Up: Parcel collected and heading to NinjaVan sorting facility.
- In Transit: Parcel moving between facilities or depots.
- Out for Delivery: Rider has parcel and will deliver today. Expect an SMS ~30 minutes before arrival.
- Delivered: Successfully delivered. Proof of delivery (photo or signature) captured.
- Failed Delivery – Attempted: Attempt made but unsuccessful. Re-attempt on next business day.
- Return to Sender (RTS): All 3 delivery attempts exhausted. Parcel being returned to shipper.

DELIVERY WINDOWS (Singapore):
- Morning: 9:00 AM – 1:00 PM
- Afternoon: 1:00 PM – 6:00 PM
- Evening: 6:00 PM – 10:00 PM
Windows are preferred — not guaranteed. You will be notified via SMS if your window cannot be met.

IF TRACKING IS NOT UPDATING:
- Allow up to 30 minutes after a scan event for the status to refresh.
- If status unchanged for more than 48 hours, contact NinjaVan support.
- During peak periods (11.11, 12.12, Eid, CNY), gaps of up to 72 hours between scans are normal.

HOW TO CONTACT SUPPORT FOR TRACKING ISSUES:
- NinjaChat Telegram: https://t.me/NinjaVanBot (Mon–Sat 9 AM–8 PM)
- Email: shippercare_sg@ninjavan.co
- Always include your tracking number.

NOTIFICATIONS:
- Automated SMS and email sent at: Picked Up, Out for Delivery, Delivered, and Failed Delivery.
- To update your contact details for notifications, contact the merchant/seller who booked the shipment. NinjaVan cannot update recipient contact details directly.

PROOF OF DELIVERY (POD):
- NinjaVan captures a photo or electronic signature at every delivery.
- POD accessible via ninjavan.co/en-sg/tracking within 24 hours of delivery.
- Merchants can bulk-download POD from dashboard.ninjavan.co.
- POD records are retained for 90 days.

TRACKING MULTIPLE PARCELS:
- Enter multiple tracking numbers separated by commas at ninjavan.co/en-sg/tracking.
- Merchants: use dashboard.ninjavan.co for bulk tracking and shipment management.
