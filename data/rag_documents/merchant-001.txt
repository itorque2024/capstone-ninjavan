Topic: For Merchants and Businesses

NinjaVan offers three service tiers for businesses. Ninja Flexi is pay-as-you-go with no account required, starting from S$3.49 per parcel. Ninja Dash is for merchants shipping more than 150 parcels per month and includes next-day delivery and custom pricing. Enterprise accounts offer custom SLAs and a dedicated account manager for high-volume shippers. The NinjaVan merchant dashboard is at dashboard.ninjavan.co and supports order creation, bulk CSV upload, API integration, real-time tracking, COD remittance reports, and proof of delivery download. Ninja Packs (prepaid poly mailers including packaging and delivery) start from S$3.40 per piece. Billing is monthly via bank transfer or PayNow. For shipper support, email shippercare_sg@ninjavan.co.
