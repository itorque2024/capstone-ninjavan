Topic: Driver Behaviour Complaints

To report a concern about a NinjaVan rider (e.g. unprofessional behaviour, parcel damaged at handover, unsafe driving), email shippercare_sg@ninjavan.co with your tracking number, the date and approximate time of the incident, and a description of what occurred. Attach photos if available. NinjaVan takes rider conduct seriously — all reports are reviewed and verified violations result in retraining or disciplinary action. For urgent safety concerns, contact support via NinjaChat (Telegram: https://t.me/NinjaVanBot) for faster response during live chat hours (Mon–Sat 9 AM–8 PM SGT).
