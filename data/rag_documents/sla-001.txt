Topic: Delivery SLA and Delays

NinjaVan's standard domestic delivery SLA for Singapore is 1–3 working days from pickup. Next-day delivery is available exclusively for Ninja Dash merchants processing more than 150 parcels per month. International delivery times vary by country (see international-comprehensive.txt for full list). Delays may occur during peak periods (11.11 Sale, 12.12 Sale, Chinese New Year, Eid al-Fitr, Christmas) or severe weather — SLA may extend by 1–3 business days during these periods. NinjaVan will proactively notify recipients of significant delays via SMS and email.
