Topic: Rescheduling Delivery — Complete Guide

HOW TO RESCHEDULE:
Option 1 — Online (self-service):
1. Go to ninjavan.co/en-sg/tracking
2. Enter your tracking number
3. Click Manage Delivery → Reschedule
4. Choose a new date (up to 7 calendar days from today)
5. Select a preferred window (Morning / Afternoon / Evening)
6. Confirm — you will receive an SMS/email confirmation

Option 2 — Contact Support:
- NinjaChat Telegram: https://t.me/NinjaVanBot (Mon–Sat 9 AM–8 PM)
- Email: shippercare_sg@ninjavan.co

RESCHEDULING RULES:
- Must be done at least 2 hours before the current scheduled delivery window.
- Cannot reschedule a parcel already Out for Delivery — the rider is on the way.
- Maximum 2 reschedules per parcel. After 2 reschedules, contact support.
- Rescheduling is free of charge.

CHANGING DELIVERY ADDRESS:
- Address changes possible before parcel is Out for Delivery.
- Must be within the same delivery zone.
- Must be arranged via support — not available via self-service.
- Contact: NinjaChat or shippercare_sg@ninjavan.co

CHANGING DELIVERY WINDOW:
- Preferred windows (Morning / Afternoon / Evening) can be chosen during rescheduling.
- Windows are prioritised, not guaranteed.
- You will be notified via SMS the morning of delivery if your preferred window cannot be met.

WHAT HAPPENS IF YOU MISS THE RESCHEDULED DELIVERY:
- NinjaVan will attempt re-delivery on the next business day.
- After 3 total failed attempts, parcel is returned to sender.
- Consider redirecting to a Ninja Point for self-collection instead.

NINJA POINT SELF-COLLECTION:
- Over 800 Ninja Points island-wide (ninjavan.co/en-sg/support/find-ninja-point).
- Bring tracking number and government-issued ID.
- Present OTP + QR code from collection notification SMS to staff.
- Returns NOT accepted at 7-Eleven locations.

PUBLIC HOLIDAYS:
- No deliveries on Singapore public holidays.
- Parcels due on a public holiday are automatically rescheduled to the next business day.
- No action needed — you will be notified.

PEAK PERIOD RESCHEDULING:
- During 11.11, 12.12, and festive seasons, rescheduling slots fill up quickly.
- Reschedule as early as possible during peak periods.
- Additional 1–2 day delays may occur even after rescheduling during very high-volume periods.
