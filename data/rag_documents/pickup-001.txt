Topic: Ninja Points and Parcel Collection

NinjaVan has over 800 Ninja Points (PUDO — Pick Up Drop Off partners) across Singapore. If you missed a delivery, you can redirect your parcel to a Ninja Point for self-collection. To find your nearest Ninja Point, visit ninjavan.co/en-sg/support/find-ninja-point and use the map with filter options (Drop-off, Collect, Return).

For collection: bring your tracking number and government-issued ID. Present the OTP and QR code from your collection notification SMS or email to Ninja Point staff.

Size limits at Ninja Points:
- Standard (most locations): under 5 kg and 80 cm combined (L+W+H)
- Medium (selected locations): under 10 kg and 120 cm
- Large (selected locations): under 20 kg and 200 cm
- Extra Large (selected locations): under 30 kg and 300 cm, no single side exceeding 150 cm

Note: Returns are NOT accepted at 7-Eleven locations. Ninja Packs (prepaid mailers) are available for purchase at selected Ninja Points.
