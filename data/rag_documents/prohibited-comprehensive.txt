Topic: Prohibited and Restricted Items

ABSOLUTELY PROHIBITED (not accepted under any circumstances):
- Explosives, detonators, fireworks, flares
- Firearms, ammunition, and weapon parts
- Illegal drugs and narcotics (including synthetic drugs)
- Human remains, organs, or biological samples
- Live animals (birds, reptiles, insects, mammals)
- Radioactive materials
- Toxic chemicals and poisons classified as hazardous
- Counterfeit goods
- Child exploitation material
- Cash and negotiable instruments (cheques, money orders)

CONDITIONALLY RESTRICTED (require special declaration and/or packaging):

Lithium batteries:
- Allowed if installed inside devices (laptops, phones, cameras)
- Standalone lithium batteries: max 100 Wh per battery, max 2 batteries per shipment
- Must be individually wrapped to prevent short-circuit
- Must be declared at time of booking

Alcohol:
- Permitted only for licensed merchants with a NinjaVan alcohol shipping agreement
- Must be packaged in leak-proof, cushioned packaging
- Cannot be shipped internationally from Singapore without an export permit

Perishables:
- Fresh food: allowed for same-day or next-day services only, with dry ice declared
- Dry ice: must be declared; max 2.5 kg per shipment; package must be ventilated
- Medication: allowed if properly labelled with prescription; not available for international shipments

High-Value Items:
- Items over S$2,000 must be declared and covered by NinjaVan Shield insurance
- Jewellery, watches, and artwork require special packaging and declaration

PACKAGING REQUIREMENTS (from NinjaVan guidelines):
- Use double-walled corrugated boxes (not poly mailers) for fragile or heavy items
- Poly mailers provide minimal protection — all contents must be individually bubble-wrapped inside
- Fragile items: minimum 8 cm of bubble wrap on all surfaces; mark FRAGILE on all sides
- Tape: minimum 2 inches wide; use H-taping method on all seams; use pressure-sensitive plastic or water-activated tape
- Do NOT use masking tape, kraft paper tape, string, or envelopes as outer packaging
- Do not bundle multiple parcels together under one label
- Liquid items: shrink-wrap spray/pump nozzles to prevent leaks

WHAT HAPPENS IF PROHIBITED ITEMS ARE DISCOVERED:
- Parcel held; sender notified
- Prohibited goods may be confiscated and handed to relevant authorities
- Sender may be liable for fines under Singapore law
- NinjaVan may terminate the account of repeat offenders

DECLARING SHIPMENT CONTENTS:
- All parcels must have accurate content descriptions on the airway bill
- Misdeclaration voids insurance coverage and is a breach of NinjaVan terms
- For international shipments, customs declaration must match actual contents
