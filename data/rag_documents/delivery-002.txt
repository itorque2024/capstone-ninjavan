Topic: Delivery Time Windows

NinjaVan delivers Monday to Saturday, 9 AM to 10 PM. Standard domestic delivery in Singapore is 1–3 working days from pickup. Next-day delivery is available for Ninja Dash shippers processing more than 150 parcels per month. Deliveries on public holidays are automatically rescheduled to the next business day. Preferred delivery windows (Morning, Afternoon, Evening) can be selected at time of booking — these are prioritised but not guaranteed. During peak periods such as 11.11 Sale, 12.12 Sale, and Chinese New Year, SLA may extend by 1–3 business days.
