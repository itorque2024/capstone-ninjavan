Topic: Filing a Damage Claim

To file a damage or loss claim, email shippercare_sg@ninjavan.co with your tracking number and photos of the damaged item and packaging. Include your purchase receipt or invoice showing item value. For electronics, also include a photo of the serial number. NinjaVan covers damage that occurred during transit. Damage due to improper packaging by the sender is not covered. Keep all original packaging until your claim is fully resolved. File promptly — late claims may not be accepted.
