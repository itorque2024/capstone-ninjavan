Topic: Parcel Size and Weight Limits

Standard NinjaVan service: up to 30 kg per parcel, no single side exceeding 150 cm, combined (L+W+H) not exceeding 300 cm.

Ninja Point drop-off size limits vary by location:
- Standard (most locations): under 5 kg and 80 cm combined (L+W+H)
- Medium (selected locations): under 10 kg and 120 cm combined
- Large (selected locations): under 20 kg and 200 cm combined
- Extra Large (selected locations): under 30 kg and 300 cm combined (no single side >150 cm)

For parcels exceeding standard limits, contact NinjaVan via shippercare_sg@ninjavan.co to arrange a bulky delivery service. Oversized or overweight parcels submitted without prior arrangement may be returned to the sender. Fragile items must be clearly labelled FRAGILE and adequately packed with a minimum 8 cm of bubble wrap on all sides inside a double-walled corrugated box.
