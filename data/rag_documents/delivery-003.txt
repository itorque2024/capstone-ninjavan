Topic: Safe Drop Policy

NinjaVan supports contactless delivery. For non-fragile, non-COD, non-high-value parcels, recipients can authorise NinjaVan to leave the parcel at a safe location (front door, reception desk, postbox) if no one is available. The rider takes a photo of the placed parcel as proof of delivery. Once the parcel is left, liability passes to the recipient. To enable Safe Drop, visit ninjavan.co/en-sg/tracking → your tracking number → Leave at Door. If you did not receive a parcel marked as safely dropped, contact NinjaVan support with your tracking number within 48 hours — the delivery photo will be shared as part of the investigation.
