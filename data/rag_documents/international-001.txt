INTERNATIONAL SHIPPING RATES:
NinjaVan uses weight-based pricing. Billing uses the higher of actual weight or volumetric weight (L × W × H in cm ÷ 5000 = volumetric kg). Indicative starting rates from Singapore:
- Malaysia: from S$5 for small parcels
- Indonesia: from S$8 for small parcels (Java/Bali); outer islands higher
- Hong Kong: from S$10 for small parcels
- Philippines: from S$8 for small parcels
- Thailand/Vietnam: from S$8 for small parcels
- Australia/UK/USA: from S$20 for small parcels
Note: Exact rates depend on parcel weight, dimensions, and service level. For a firm quote, visit ninjavan.co or email shippercare_sg@ninjavan.co.

DDP (Delivered Duty Paid) is available for Malaysia, Indonesia, Philippines, Thailand, and Vietnam — the sender pays import duties upfront, so recipients do not face surprise charges on delivery.

Customs duties and import taxes are the recipient's responsibility unless DDP is selected. Prohibited items (weapons, live animals, hazardous materials) cannot be shipped. All international shipments require an accurate customs declaration form — misdeclaration voids insurance coverage and may result in legal liability.
