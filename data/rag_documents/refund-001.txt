Topic: Refund Policy

Refunds for products are issued by the seller, not NinjaVan. NinjaVan is the delivery partner and does not process product refunds or take-back payments. For product refunds, contact the seller directly. NinjaVan can assist with return shipping logistics if the seller has arranged a return order. For NinjaVan service compensation (e.g. lost or damaged parcel during transit), email shippercare_sg@ninjavan.co with your tracking number and proof of purchase. Approved service compensation claims are paid via bank transfer. NinjaVan Shield insurance (purchased at time of booking by the sender) covers up to S$2,000 for lost or damaged parcels.
