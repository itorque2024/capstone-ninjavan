Topic: Cash on Delivery (COD) — Full Policy

WHAT IS COD:
Cash on Delivery means the recipient pays at the door when the parcel is delivered, rather than paying online in advance. NinjaVan collects payment on the merchant's behalf.

HOW COD WORKS FOR RECIPIENTS:
1. You place an order with the COD option selected.
2. NinjaVan rider arrives with your parcel.
3. You pay the stated amount.
4. Rider hands over the parcel.

ACCEPTED PAYMENT METHODS FOR COD:
- Cash (SGD)
- PayNow QR code (for participating merchants — rider displays QR on device)
Note: Credit/debit cards are NOT accepted at the door. Prepare exact cash where possible.

FAILED COD DELIVERY:
- If you cannot pay the COD amount at delivery, the attempt is marked as failed.
- The parcel will be re-attempted on the next business day.
- After 3 failed COD attempts, the parcel is returned to the sender.
- Contact the merchant to arrange alternative payment before the next attempt.

PARTIAL PAYMENT:
- NinjaVan does not accept partial COD payment. The full stated amount must be paid.
- If the amount shown is incorrect, do not accept the parcel — contact the merchant immediately.

FOR MERCHANTS — COD REMITTANCE:
- COD collected is consolidated at end of the month and remitted to the merchant via bank transfer or PayNow after calculations and verification.
- Handling fee: S$1 OR 3% of the COD invoice value, whichever is higher — deducted from COD before remittance.
- COD collection statuses are viewable on the NinjaVan merchant dashboard (dashboard.ninjavan.co).
- Failed COD collections are reported in the shipment report on the dashboard.

COD RECEIPT:
- Always request and keep the COD payment receipt as proof of payment.
