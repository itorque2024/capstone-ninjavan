Topic: Escalation Process

If your issue has not been resolved after contacting NinjaVan support, you can request escalation to a senior support agent. To escalate: reply to your existing support conversation and include the words ESCALATE, your case reference number, and a clear description of the unresolved issue. Senior agents handle escalations within 1 business day. Contact NinjaVan support via NinjaChat (Telegram: https://t.me/NinjaVanBot, Mon–Sat 9 AM–8 PM SGT) or email shippercare_sg@ninjavan.co. For scam-related incidents involving NinjaVan's name, NinjaVan works with the Singapore Police Force — report scams to the SPF and notify NinjaVan via shippercare_sg@ninjavan.co.
