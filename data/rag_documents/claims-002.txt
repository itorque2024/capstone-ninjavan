Topic: Lost Parcel Claim

If your parcel has not been delivered and tracking has not updated for more than 5 business days, it may be lost. Email shippercare_sg@ninjavan.co with your tracking number and proof of purchase to initiate a lost parcel investigation. Include a description of the contents and their value. NinjaVan will conduct an internal trace investigation. If the parcel is confirmed lost, NinjaVan will compensate up to the declared value. For higher-value parcels, NinjaVan Shield insurance (purchased at time of booking) covers up to S$2,000 or declared value, whichever is lower.
